FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the immigration process take?

Processing times vary by visa type and country. Tourist visas can take 2-4 weeks, student visas 4-12 weeks, work permits 6-16 weeks, and PR applications 6-18 months. We provide estimated timelines for each case during the initial consultation.

What is your success rate?

We maintain an industry-leading success rate of 98%. Our high success rate is attributed to thorough profile assessment before taking on cases, meticulous documentation, and experienced case officers who handle each application with utmost care.

Do you offer free consultation?

Yes! We offer a completely free initial consultation where our experts assess your profile, discuss your goals, and recommend the best visa options. You can book your free consultation via WhatsApp, phone, or by visiting our office.

What documents do I need?

Documents vary by visa type but generally include passport, educational certificates, work experience letters, language test scores (IELTS/PTE), financial documents, and photographs. We provide a detailed customized checklist after your initial assessment.

What are your fees?

Our fees are transparent with no hidden charges. We provide a complete fee breakdown including our professional charges and government fees before you commit. We also offer flexible payment plans. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you have a refund policy?

Yes, we have a transparent refund policy. If your visa application is refused due to reasons within our control, we offer a full refund of our professional fees. Government fees are non-refundable as per government policies. Complete terms are shared in the service agreement.
