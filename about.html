Our Story

Building Dreams Since 2009

GlobalVisa Immigration Services was founded with a single mission: to make the complex world of immigration simple, transparent, and accessible for everyone.

What started as a small consultancy in New Delhi has grown into one of India's most trusted immigration firms, with offices across major cities and a team of 50+ certified immigration professionals.

Over the past 15+ years, we have successfully processed more than 50,000 visa applications across 25+ countries, maintaining an industry-leading success rate of 98%. Our commitment to ethical practices, transparent pricing, and personalized service has earned us the trust of thousands of families.
